16.1
水無月の虚空に涼し時鳥

minazuki no
kokū ni suzushi
hototogisu

In the coolness
of the empty sixth-month sky...
the cuckoo's cry.


48.1    
木をつみて夜の明やすき小窓かな
        
ki o tsumite
yo no akeyasuki
komado kana
        
the tree cut,
dawn breaks early
at my little window

49.1    
一重づゝ一重つゝ散れ八重櫻
        
hitoezutsu
hitoezutsu chire
yaezakura
        
scatter layer
by layer, eight-layered
cherry blossoms!
49.2    
名月の出るやゆらめく花薄
        
meigetsu no
deru ya yurameku
hanasusuki
        
at the full moon's
rising, the silver-plumed
reeds tremble
50.1    
ちる花にもつるゝ鳥の翼かな
        
chiru hana ni
motsururu tori no
tsubasa kana
        
entangled with
the scattering cherry blossoms—
the wings of birds!
